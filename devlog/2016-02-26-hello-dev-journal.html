Hi new dev journal!

Problems

  • I do lots of interesting stuff that I never tell a soul about. I'd like to practice creating a narrative around my creations
  • Often, when I do write about my work, the writing act takes me away from what I was doing.

This is a place for

  • Logging whatever I'm working on at the moment
  • Quick brain dumps
  • Jotting down ideas without worrying about editing

More than anything, it's my intention to cultivate a practice of journaling.

It is not a place for

  • Careful editing and indepth posts
  • A consistently cohesive narrative

Process rules

Don't meta-analyze much. Just write down your thoughts and move on.

If you feel like this might be a fruitful topic to write about in more depth, add it to a list of post ideas. Set aside time once or twice a week for in-depth writing, and choose one topic for each session.

Some secondary purposes

These posts might end up being useful post-creation, but need not be:

  • Mining my own ideas for other work
  • Sharing out, to get feedback from friends and colleagues.

but its primary purpose is to provide a space for cultivating a practice of journaling.

This is a place for me to talk about whatever I'm working on at the moment.

Design principles

  • Optimize for journaling
  • also looking back on recent history
  • and searching

Avoid

  • Fancy headers/templates. Keep the templates as simple as possible.

I'm often reluctant to post process stuff directly to social media outlets like Twitter and Facebook, but feel much more comfortable posting the primary source on ato a site I control, and then sharing links to different outlets.
